I decided to create my own web extension[^1], because of annoyingness of new Google's policy popups/redirectors. My Firefox is set to not save cookies after closing it. So upon entering any webpage I'm ready to get welcomed by some kind of accept cookies popup. Although what Google did, made me spend some of my spare time to create an extension to kill their popups. To see youtube's home page, you need to click four buttons! **That's nuts!**
