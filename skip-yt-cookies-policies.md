I decided to create my own web extension[^1], because of annoyingness of new Google's policy popups/redirectors. My Firefox is set to not save cookies after closing it. So upon entering any webpage I'm ready to get welcomed by some kind of accept cookies window. Although what Google did, made me spend some of my spare time to create an simple extension to kill their popups. In order to see YouTube's home page, you have to click four buttons! **That's nuts!**
